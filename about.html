Club History

2018-current

Beginnings

In 2018, Sun point travelling Co.Ltd formed a club named "Sun Point Rally Club" as the owner is a huge motorsports enthusiaist. The club started out as an motorsports enthusiast club that do car meetups.

Ceo of Sun Point Travelling Co.Ltd
Sun point Rally club
2018-2020

Rebrand

Starting from 2019, sun point rally club holds rally stages across the globe every year. Rally challenges are for members-only and our team plans routes and events for the club members to enjoy.
In 2020, The club started to gain more popularity after it rebranded to "Taffad rally club".

What We do

We provide services for anyone who wants to enjoy the scenery and compete in famous rally stages around the world. One of the services is car transportation via cargo plane or by sea depending on the location. Hotel room and plane tickets are also included in members package.

Our Team

Our Team's goal is to let fans enjoy the famous rally stages around the world safely regardless of their experience. Our team research the rally stages and plans the route in each challenges for impeccable experience.
So, Join us to rally across the world.
