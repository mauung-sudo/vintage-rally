Top Questions with answers

How much is a ticket to a single rally stage?
Sorry for the inconvience as the ticket information isn't currently available on the website. You can contact us at 1-800-975-8077 about ticket info.
Can I be member without vintage car?
Yes, you can. Everyone is welcome to be a member.
Are non-rally-build cars allowed?
Yes, these rally stages are not for who gets 1st in each stage but rather for fans to experience famous rally stages like Whaanga Coast.
Can I compete without a vintage rally car?
If you want to compete in rally stages around the world, a car manufactured before the year 2000 is required.
Are cars shown in home page actually actually competed in 80s 90s rally?
No, they didn't take part in 80s and 90s rally. They are replicas built in the exact specifications.
Are only experienced rally drivers allowed to compete?
No, everyone with a car manufactured before the year 2000 can compete. You can even modify your car with modern technologies if you like.
